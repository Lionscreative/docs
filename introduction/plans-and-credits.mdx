## How Credits Work

### Daily Credit Claims

Every user can claim **5 free credits** once every 24 hours.

- **Claim frequency**: Once per 24 hours
- **Credits per claim**: 5 credits
- **Monthly limit (Free plan)**: 30 claims (150 credits potential)
- **Credits accumulate**: They don't expire and roll over

<Info>
  Pro and Enterprise users get monthly credits in addition to daily claims.
</Info>

### Credit Consumption

Credit usage varies based on the complexity of your request:

| Action | Estimated Credits |
|--------|-------------------|
| Simple styling change | 1-3 credits |
| New component | 5-15 credits |
| Full page creation | 15-30 credits |
| Complex feature | 30-50 credits |

*Actual usage depends on the AI response length and complexity.*

### What Happens When You Run Out?

- Your apps continue to work normally
- You can't make new AI-assisted changes until you get more credits
- Options to get more credits:
  - Wait and claim your next daily credits
  - Upgrade to a paid plan
  - Refer friends to earn bonus credits

## Referral Program

Earn bonus credits by inviting friends:

| Who | Credits Earned |
|-----|----------------|
| **You (referrer)** | +15 credits when they publish their first app |
| **Your friend (referee)** | +10 credits on signup |

<Warning>
  Referral credits are capped at 25 credits total per referrer.
</Warning>

### How to Refer

1. Go to **Settings** in your dashboard
2. Copy your unique referral link
3. Share it with friends
4. They sign up and you both earn credits

## Free Actions

These actions don't consume credits:

- Viewing and browsing your apps
- Browsing template gallery
- Managing account settings
- Viewing version history
- Publishing to Vercel (deployment is free)
- Connecting custom domains
- Using the file browser and code viewer
- Team collaboration features

## Billing FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What happens when I run out of credits?">
    Your apps continue to work normally. You just can't make new AI-assisted changes until you get more credits. You can claim your next daily credits or upgrade your plan.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do unused credits expire?">
    No! Your credits accumulate and never expire. Daily claims add to your balance.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I cancel anytime?">
    Yes. Cancel anytime from Settings. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I claim daily credits?">
    Visit your dashboard and click the "Claim Credits" button. You can claim once every 24 hours.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a limit on daily claims?">
    Free users can make up to 30 daily claims per month. Paid users have no limit on daily claims.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
