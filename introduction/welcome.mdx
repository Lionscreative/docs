Kleap is an AI-powered platform that lets you build and deploy professional web applications through natural conversation. Simply describe what you want, and watch your app come to life.

## What is Kleap?

Kleap combines the power of advanced AI with modern web technologies to help you:

- **Build faster**: Go from idea to deployed app in minutes
- **No coding required**: Describe your app in plain language
- **Production-ready**: Deploy to your own domain with one click
- **Full-stack**: Database, authentication, and hosting included

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="AI-Powered Development">
    Describe your app in natural language. Our AI understands context and generates clean, production-ready code using Next.js, React, and Tailwind CSS.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Ready-to-Use Starter">
    Start with a pre-configured Next.js setup including Supabase, Tailwind, and shadcn/ui - ready to customize with AI.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Live Preview">
    See your changes instantly as you build. Real-time preview with hot reload keeps you in the flow.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="One-Click Deploy">
    Deploy to Vercel with a single click. Connect your custom domain and go live in seconds.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Built-in Database">
    Supabase integration gives you a PostgreSQL database, user authentication, and file storage out of the box.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
