Understanding the limits of Kleap and integrated services.

## Kleap Limits

### Credits

| Plan | Daily Claims | Monthly Credits | Claim Limit |
|------|--------------|-----------------|-------------|
| **Free** | 5 per claim | - | 30 claims/month |
| **Starter** | 5 per claim | Included | Unlimited |
| **Pro** | 5 per claim | Included | Unlimited |
| **Enterprise** | 5 per claim | Unlimited | Unlimited |

### How Daily Claims Work

- Claim once every 24 hours
- Each claim adds 5 credits to your balance
- Credits accumulate and never expire
- Free users limited to 30 claims per month

### Collaboration

## Preview (Sandbox) Limits

### Sandbox Resources

| Resource | Limit |
|----------|-------|
| Memory | 2 GB |
| CPU | Shared |
| Disk Space | 10 GB |
| Auto-stop | 15 min idle |

### Sandbox Behavior

- Auto-stops after 15 minutes of inactivity
- Restarts automatically when you return
- State (code, files) preserved
- Browser sessions lost on restart
- Preview URL format: `https://3000-{id}.proxy.daytona.works`

<Info>
  The auto-stop timeout is configurable and helps conserve resources.
</Info>

## Hitting Limits

### What Happens

- **Credits exhausted**: Can't make AI changes until next claim or upgrade
- **App limit reached**: Can't publish new apps until you upgrade
- **Vercel limits**: Deployment may fail or traffic throttled
- **Supabase limits**: Database operations may fail

### Solutions

1. **Claim daily credits** - Free users can claim 5 credits every 24 hours
2. **Upgrade plan** - More credits and higher limits
3. **Wait for reset** - Some limits reset monthly
4. **Optimize usage** - Write efficient queries, compress images
