## Preview (Sandbox) Limits

### Sandbox Resources

| Resource | Limit |
|----------|-------|
| Memory | 2 GB |
| CPU | Shared |
| Disk Space | 10 GB |
| Timeout | 15 min idle |

### Sandbox Behavior

- Auto-stops after 15 minutes of inactivity
- Restarts automatically on interaction
- State (code, files) preserved
- Browser sessions lost on restart

## Hitting Limits

### What Happens

- **Credits exhausted**: Can't make AI changes until reset
- **Vercel limits**: Deployment may fail or traffic limited
- **Supabase limits**: Database operations may fail

### Solutions

1. **Upgrade plan** - More resources available
2. **Optimize usage** - Write efficient queries, compress images
3. **Wait for reset** - Monthly limits reset on billing cycle
