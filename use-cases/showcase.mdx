## Submit Your App

We love featuring community projects! To be considered:

### Requirements

- Built primarily with Kleap
- Publicly accessible
- Represents quality work
- You have rights to share

### How to Submit

1. **Email** hello@kleap.co with:
   - App URL
   - Brief description
   - Screenshots
   - Your name/handle for credit

2. **Or share on Discord** in #showcase channel
